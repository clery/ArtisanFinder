# ArtisanFinder

## [v1.2.6](https://github.com/clery/ArtisanFinder/releases/tag/v1.2.6) (2026-05-22)

[Full Changelog](https://github.com/clery/ArtisanFinder/compare/v1.2.5...v1.2.6) | [Previous Releases](https://github.com/clery/ArtisanFinder/releases)

### Changed

* ArtisanFinder now keeps saved craft data leaner by avoiding unnecessary scan internals.

### Fixed

* Craft data now stays consistent across client languages.
* Unsupported profession systems such as gathering, secondary, and tertiary professions are no longer scanned or shown in crafter advertising rows.
* ArtisanFinder's crafter panel is now hidden for unsupported professions.
